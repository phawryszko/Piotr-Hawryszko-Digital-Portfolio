About Me

Hi there. My name is Piotr and I'm a photographer and designer. I am 25 years old and I am originally from Poland. For the better part of my life, I studied IT to work in technical suport. IT Technicians are in demand and are paid handsomely. For years I was driven by the thought that great pay check can outweigh the cons of any job and after working several summer jobs that made me despise every second of each 8 hour shift, I realised how insignificant money is. Right there and then I decided that I want to go to work with a smile on my face instead of thinking up excuses to call in sick. IT was not the way to go for me. The decision to take up my hobbies and turn them into a full time job came in the summer of 2013. I sent out applications to universities every year since 2010. Every year I picked a different course and ended up working full time anyway. At the time I thought my inability to make a decision is going to lead me nowhere but looking at it now, not making a decision was the best decision that I made. Now in my 2nd year of study of Digital Arts at University of Kent, I am truly happy with my choice. Photography, Web Design and Graphic Design have always fascinated me but my desire to pursue them as a career was deterred by the thought of not making enough money. In the summer of 2015 I decided to become fully equipped to pursue what I love. After working 12-16 hour shifts, 7 days a week, I was able to buy myself a brand new laptop, a DSLR with a range of lenses and a Wacom tablet. Now that nothing stands in the way of my creativity, I present to you a portfolio of my work gathered over a very short period of time. This is the time to put my career back on track before it even took off.

Piotr HawryszkoPhotographer & Designer
